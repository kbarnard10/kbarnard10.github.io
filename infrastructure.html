Infrastructure: On-premise or Hosted?

  • Hosted Solutions
  • On-Premise Solutions
  • Build vs. Buy

Now that you understand authentication and authorization, you will need to decide whether you want to find a hosted solution or build your own with an on-premise solution. Yes, there are hosted solutions for this! Yes, you could also build this out on your own! But which is best? Well, it depends on your business needs, timeframes, security requirements, how often you want to maintain authentication and authorization, overhead, and budget. Just remember, since it’s technology, what has worked in the past does not always work in today’s context.

Why Hosted?

  • Speed to market and less overhead - frees up your time to focus on the core product
  • Security - Let a trusted team handle security requirements for you and to maintain them moving forward.
  • Scalability and High Performance - Hosted solutions offer guaranteed SLAs and can handle large amounts of users. Pick a solution that offers 99.999% uptime and can handle as many users as you possibly might have!
  • Maintenance and enhancements - technology changes pretty often and so do ways to authenticate into a web or mobile application.
  • Fixed costs - No need to worry about hidden fees or overhead costs.

Why On-Prem?
  • Strict security requirements - If you have very unique compliance requirements, you can ensure that you build out your solution to fulfill all requirements
  • Complex unique use cases very specific to your industry
  • No internet access
  • Sufficient resources - You have a database, servers, backup strategy, firewall, and necessary expertise on staff (security team, developers, architect) and sufficient resources to provision what is required. You are
  • confident you can maintain this moving forward.
  • Flexible and open budget
